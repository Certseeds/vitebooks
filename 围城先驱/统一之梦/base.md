# 围城先驱-统一之梦-序章

作者: Nike Kyme

On the Cover: In the depth of the Terran underhive, legendary warriors from the time of Unity fight for survival

## 序言

> > 吾等即雷霆，Weare the thunder,
>
> > 吾等乃闪电，we are the lightning,
